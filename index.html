Under New Management!

 As of April, 2015, The cottages are under new management. As the new managers, we promise to provide excellent service as well as a clean and friendly atmosphere. We sincerely hope you will visit us. We know that you will enjoy your stay.

A place for family and friends.

 The cottages sit on the edge of Carlyle lake's shore in Eldon Hazlet State Park. This wonderful location offers scenic views and a relaxing locale. Whether you are looking for a weekend full of fishing or a romantic getaway in the middle of the week, these cottages will suit your every need. Each cottage comes equipped with a full bathroom, a kitchenette, two sleeping areas, and a cozy living space, complete with complimentary sattelite television.

 Within the state park there is a fully-stocked camp store which strives to provide you with anything you may need for your stay in the beautiful state park. Next to the camp store is a large public swimming pool with a certified lifeguard. The cottages are available for booking all year round and offer heating and air conditioning to ensure your stay is comfortable regardless of the weather outside.
